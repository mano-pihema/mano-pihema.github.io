What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

That i can get frustrated pretty quickly when i get stuck. Learning to recognize and try to neutralise it has been valuable. That I don’t like asking for help, still something i'm working on. Being told to take it easy on the self criticism has revealed how normal it was for me to focus negatively, getting out of that habit will be well worth it.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

The above human skills have huge roles given the actual working environment in the industry will be working closely in teams.

Just the overall importance it plays in securing a job. I kinda assumed it was not at the same level of importance as the practical coding skills but i'm now starting to recognise its at least level with these coding skill we are learning too.
