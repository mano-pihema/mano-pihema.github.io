Research Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

Understanding and taking on board the ideas of neuroplasticity removes the limits that people may have previously placed on themselves.For instance doubt about whether they are able to do some task , such as drawing. When it is understood that the brain is adaptable, hopefully that person resolves to carry on learning to draw, as it is only a matter of time and practise.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit.

Forming new beneficial habits is the most basic and effective means of using neuroplasticity to your own advantage. One example might be getting to bed by a certain time. As this is practised over the long term, it becomes normal and the full host of benefits that come with being well rested are reaped.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

Creating a stimulating environment for your brain is the best way to increase neuroplasticity. Challenge the brain by taking up activities such as learning a new language, learning an instrument, or doing a sudoku.

Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

Growth mindset is an outlook that does not shy away from hard work, and in fact understands that hard work is the path to mastery. They remain curious and motivated in the face of challenging tasks. Seeing these tasks as an opportunity to learn and grow. Feedback will be seen as constructive and not as an attack.
